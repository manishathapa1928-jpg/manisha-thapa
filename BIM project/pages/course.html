Bachelor Of Information Management

The Bachelor of Information Management is a four-year eight semester program of the faculty of management, tribhuwan university offering integrated information Technology and Management Courses. This course is envisioned to enable students to develop skills in information technology, and the apply the theories and concept of the broad integrated curriculum covering computing, business fundamentals and critical analytical decision making.
